About Us

Our mission is to save children around the world from tobacco by affecting social and cultural attitudes towards tobacco use.

We believe that communicating our message through technology helps to reach more people because youth readily technology.

We specifically target youth because they have not yet become addicted to tobacco.

Currently, our main focus is on Africa and Asia because these regions have the least government participation in tobacco cessation programs.
